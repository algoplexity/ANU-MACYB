*A unifying research frame from simple CPS prototype to doctoral-level theory*

---

## 1. Conceptual Core

**Mesoscope**: an instrument that operates between micro and macro scales.

* Microscope → component-level physics
* Telescope → macro statistics
* Mesoscope → regime structure, coordination dynamics, rule transitions

The **Algorithmic Mesoscope** is a cybernetic instrument that:

1. Observes multi-scale dynamics in a CPS.
2. Encodes them symbolically and latently.
3. Detects regime shifts via algorithmic compression or causal rule change.
4. Feeds back adaptive control.

It integrates:

* CPS embodiment (physical artifact + sensing + actuation)
* Complexity economics (regime shifts, emergent order)
* Cybernetics (Viable System Model, recursive control, feedback)
* AI fluency (human–AI co-adaptation, interpretability layers)
